# Pula Labs Pitch Deck Narrative Plan

Audience: Startup India reviewers, early-stage investors, and strategic partners evaluating whether Pula Labs addresses a real SME/MSME operations opportunity.

Objective: Create a clear 12-slide pitch deck that follows the Startup India pitch deck guideline PDF while staying honest about missing founder-supplied numbers.

Narrative arc: Indian business owners are still stuck between spreadsheets, disconnected tools, and generic dashboards. Pula Labs gives them one adaptive workspace for CRM, billing, inventory, teams, operations, and optional AI. The deck moves from pain to product, then business model, market, traction placeholders, financial assumptions, funding ask, cap table, and exit logic.

Slide list:
1. Cover: company, URL, one-line concept, contact placeholders.
2. Team: roles, contribution, equity placeholders.
3. Pain Point: decision fatigue, disconnected operations, poor visibility, slow follow-ups.
4. Product / Technology: adaptive workspace modules and AI layer.
5. Business Model: subscription plans, AI credits, onboarding/training, implementation path.
6. Market Opportunity: Indian MSME/SME focus with a replaceable TAM/SAM/SOM model.
7. Current Traction: product built, dashboard modules, pricing/trial flow, event/customer placeholder.
8. Competitive Landscape: alternatives and Pula Labs's plan to win without an overclaiming matrix.
9. Financials: 3-year illustrative planning model and unit economics placeholders.
10. Funding Needs: amount, runway, use of funds, milestones, follow-on capital placeholders.
11. Equity Structure: cap table and fundraising history placeholders.
12. Exit Options: acquisition, consolidation, vertical SaaS expansion, and closing ask.

Source plan: Use the Startup India guideline PDF accessed by URL, local product code in `src/components` and `src/dashboard`, and the local Pula Labs logo in `public/pulalabs-logo.png`. Treat all unknown business figures as placeholders instead of fabricating exact values.

Visual system: Investor-clean SaaS deck, off-white background, charcoal text, teal primary, saffron and green accents, small operational UI mockups, compact tables, and restrained diagrams. Keep all visible pitch copy editable in PowerPoint.

Asset needs: Pula Labs logo from the repository. No generated image plates are required because the deck is product and data led, with visuals composed from editable shapes, tables, charts, and the real logo asset.

Editability plan: Titles, bullets, tables, diagrams, financial numbers, and placeholders remain editable PowerPoint objects. The only raster asset is the logo.
